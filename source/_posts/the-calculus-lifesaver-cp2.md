#### 三角恒等式
$$\cos(x) = \sin(\frac{\pi}{2} - x)$$
$$\tan(x) = \cot(\frac{\pi}{2} - x)$$
$$\sec(x) = \csc(\frac{\pi}{2} - x)$$

$$\sin(A+B) = \sin(A) \cos(B) + \cos(A) \sin(B)$$
$$\cos(A+B) = \cos(A) \cos(B) - \sin(A) \sin(B)$$

#### 倍角公式
$$\sin(2x) = 2\sin(x)\cos(x)$$
$$\cos(2x) = 2\cos^2(x) - 1 = 1 - 2sin^2(x)$$
